About my pottery

I started in 2021, working under Robin Jenkins who taught me everything I know, and who's studio I continue to be welcomed back to. I love making teapots, but mugs make the best gifts and let me explore my creativity the most. Since I am a fulltime student at McGill University in Montreal, I am only able to create pottery from May through August. I plan to restock my Etsy shop in June with more star mugs and some other new stuff! Sorry to all my lovely dedicated customers for the wait.
